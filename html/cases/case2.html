Cloud-based 3D image modeling enhances
the design efficiency of distributed solar
photovoltaic systems

More precise, thoughtful, and effective 3D visualization solutions for photovoltaic design.

More accurate, intelligent, and efficient 3D visualization solutions for photovoltaic design. In the photovoltaic (PV) industry, data is typically collected through drone aerial photography and on-site surveys. Subsequently, the shadow range, the PV panels' positioning, direction, inclination, and other relevant information are manually recorded to finalize the program vector drawing. Eventually, professional software is acquired for model design and layout rendering. This entire process is time-consuming and labor-intensive. Get3D Cloud, an online modeling platform, offers accurate, intelligent, and efficient 3D visualization solutions for PV module arrangements, PV system designs, and optimizations by generating high-precision, real-life 3D models of PV sites and conducting intelligent shadow light analysis applicable to both centralized and distributed PV application scenarios.

Automatic modeling

By uploading the images taken by the drone in Get3D Cloud, a high-precision 3D model of the area can be automatically generated. The model's accuracy is better than 5cm, and it carries geographic information such as latitude, longitude, elevation, etc., which can provide accurate 3D visualization data for measuring the distance, elevation difference, and house area at a later stage.

Shadow analysis

Shadow shading significantly affects the operational lifespan of the PV system, overall energy production, and revenue generation. Therefore, during the pre-construction phase, we must calculate the shadow shading extent of the area where the power plant will be situated.

The high-precision 3D model created by Get3D Cloud accurately depicts the shapes and locations of buildings, trees, and other obstacles (such as walls, chimneys, water heaters, and exhaust systems) in the real environment. It considers the height, shape, and arrangement of buildings, the types of trees, and their growth patterns, allowing for a more accurate simulation of the sun’s path and shadow creation. This model visually represents shadow distribution, providing trustworthy shadow analysis results that help schedule and install PV modules by illustrating shadows' position, extent, and duration.

Multi types of results and multi-terminal display

Get3D Cloud integrates data from various sources, supports BIM models and CAD data overlays, and enables information interoperability between geographic scenes and engineering design. Project management facilitates a multi-period timeline comparison and dual-screen roll-up comparison, allowing project personnel to visualize the changes in the project area over time, understand the project situation, and meet project reporting and management requirements.

Additionally, Get3D Cloud features built-in lightweight technology, enabling the 3D lightweight display of PV design solutions on mobile devices (tablet PCs, cell phones). This feature significantly enhances the convenience of project reporting, results display, and project acceptance, empowering users with on-the-go access to their projects.

With Get3D Cloud's 3D reconstruction and analysis capabilities, users can conduct one-stop operations such as building measurement, shadow analysis, irradiation analysis, module layout, electrical configuration, and result output. This comprehensive suite of features enhances the overall efficiency of PV design and project implementation, providing users with a reliable and robust platform for their needs.
