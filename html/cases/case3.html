Application of 3D digital technology in mine management

3D visualization models offer practical and scientific solutions for mining engineering.

3D visualization models offer scientific and efficient solutions for mining engineering.

In the past, mining operations relied heavily on human labor and were inefficient. With Get3D Cloud’s cloud-based 3D reconstruction and analysis technology, we can create an intricate 3D model of the mining area to visualize the topography, geological features, and distribution of mineral resources, providing scientific data for the design of the mining plan.

Cloud-based piercing point modeling for creating highly accurate models

The 12,072 photos of the mining area collected by the drone were uploaded to Get3D Cloud, where 3D reconstruction was conducted using the “RTK positioning + cloud-piercing point" function. The photo inclusion rate reached 100%, and the average error of model accuracy was within 1 cm, meeting the accuracy requirements for 3D modeling in mines.

Multiple data outputs for scientific analysis

Get3D Cloud generates OSGB and OBJ model data and automatically creates DSM (Digital Surface Model) and DOM (Digital Orthophoto Model) for mining analysis. By importing the DSM into GIS software and extracting the topographic contours, we can assess mine resource reserves to determine the construction scale and illustrate the construction plan's feasibility and soundness.

Digital Marking and Calculation for Effective Planning

Users can digitally annotate the model's points, lines, and surfaces in Get3D Cloud. The volume calculation function quickly determines the excavation volume, and the elevation calculation function efficiently calculates the local elevation for practical mine construction and operation planning.

Multi-Temporal Comparative Analysis for Restoring Green Ecology

Through Get3D Cloud, users can visualize real-life 3D models of mines before and after ecological restoration on a computer screen. This technology intuitively, three-dimensionally, and realistically demonstrates the impact of mining activities on the surrounding environment and shows the state of the mines post-restoration. It provides objective and transparent scientific evaluation support for constructing green mines while serving as a foundation for project acceptance.

With these capabilities, Get3D Cloud offers a comprehensive, one-stop solution for mine management, enabling scientific decision-making and efficient oversight of mining operations.
