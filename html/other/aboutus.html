About US

A technology company specializing in
reconstructing, understanding, and applying
real-world 3D models.

Contact Us

The technology is rooted in academic research conducted at the ETH Zurich and University of Stuttgart, and the founding team has experience working at ETH Zurich, Microsoft Research, Wuhan University and the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

At Get3d, our technical team, with over 20 years of experience, is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of 3D technology. We emphasize research and innovation as the foundation of our existence. Our commitment to advancing the evolution of spatial information technology and delivering maximum value to our customers is unwavering.

We will continue to research and develop solutions and services for sensing, modeling, computation, and application of the physical world, inspiring the potential of 3D technology.

Who are we?

Our technology finds applications across various fields, delivering essential
3D data support to the industry.

Our products cater to multiple sectors, showcasing our versatility and expertise. We are a team dedicated to fundamental research equipped with technical expertise in photogrammetry, mapping, and GIS. Our technology has been utilized at many renowned archaeological excavation sites, digitizing world heritage locations, 3D reconstructing megacities, contributing to large-scale engineering projects, and enhancing space exploration.
